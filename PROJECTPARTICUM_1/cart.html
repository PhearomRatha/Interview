    BMW X5: EXCELLENT TRACTION WITH xOFFROAD PACKAGE.

    xSand.

    The following parameters are preset with the off-road mode "xSand": additional ground clearance 20 mm, off-road ABS can be activated from a speed of 10 km/h, readily controllable response from the accelerator pedal. The result: optimum traction even on unsurfaced roads.

    xGravel.

    If you activate "xGravel" mode, the following parameters are preset: additional ground clearance 20 mm, off-road-ABS can be activated immediately, comfortable response from the accelerator pedal. The result: perfect balance of smoothness and safety on loose, stony ground.
